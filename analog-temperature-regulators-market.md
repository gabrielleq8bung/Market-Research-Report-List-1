Thermoelectric regulators utilize the Peltier effect to achieve precise temperature control by transferring heat effectively, ideal for cooling and heating small devices. The "Other" category includes alternative technologies that may incorporate mechanical or hybrid systems, catering to niche applications requiring specialized temperature management solutions.</p></p>
