<p><p>The chemical process pumps market is categorized into monopole and double pole types. Monopole pumps are single-shaft systems primarily designed for lower capacity applications, offering simplicity and ease of installation. In contrast, double pole pumps feature dual shafts, enhancing operational efficiency and allowing for higher capacity handling. Both types are engineered to handle aggressive chemicals and varying viscosities, ensuring reliability and longevity in chemical processing environments, making them essential for industries like petrochemicals and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
