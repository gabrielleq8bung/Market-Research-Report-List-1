<p><strong>The Pipe Bursting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pipe Bursting System</li><li>Cable-Pull Pipe Bursting System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pipe Bursting System market comprises two primary types: Hydraulic Pipe Bursting Systems and Cable-Pull Pipe Bursting Systems. Hydraulic Pipe Bursting utilizes hydraulic pressure to fracture existing pipes, enabling the installation of new ones simultaneously. This method is efficient for larger diameter pipes and offers minimal surface disruption. In contrast, Cable-Pull Pipe Bursting employs a cable mechanism to pull the new pipe through the burst section, ideal for smaller diameter pipes, providing flexibility in urban environments with limited access.</p></p>
