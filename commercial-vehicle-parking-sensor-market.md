<p><p>The commercial vehicle parking sensor market comprises two main types: forward sensors and rearview sensors. Forward parking sensors are designed to detect obstacles in front of the vehicle, aiding drivers during parking maneuvers and preventing collisions. Rearview sensors, on the other hand, focus on monitoring the area behind the vehicle, providing alerts to avoid accidents while reversing. Both types enhance safety, improve maneuverability in tight spaces, and are increasingly adopted in various commercial vehicles for better operational efficiency and driver confidence.</p></p>
