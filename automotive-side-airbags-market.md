<p><ul><li>Side Torso Airbags</li><li>Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side airbags market comprises two main types: side torso airbags and side tubular (or curtain) airbags. Side torso airbags are designed to protect the upper body, typically deployed from the seat or door to cushion the impact during a side collision. In contrast, side tubular airbags deploy from the roof area, covering the side windows to protect occupants' heads from impact and ejection. Both types enhance passenger safety by minimizing injuries during side-impact accidents.</p></p>
