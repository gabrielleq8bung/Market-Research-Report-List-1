<p><p>The semiconductor vacuum pump market encompasses several key types, each suited for specific applications. Dry pumps utilize a completely oil-free mechanism, ensuring cleanliness, while multi-stage roots pumps achieve high vacuum levels through efficient gas compression. Turbomolecular pumps leverage high-speed rotor blades to create strong vacuum environments, ideal for delicate silicon wafer processing. Claw pumps operate with minimal maintenance and low noise, making them suitable for environments where cleanliness and reliability are crucial, such as semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
