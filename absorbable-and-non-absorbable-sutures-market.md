<p><p>Absorbable and non-absorbable sutures are critical components in surgical procedures. Latest trends include the development of innovative materials that enhance biocompatibility and reduce infection rates, along with the increasing adoption of automated suturing devices. These factors combined point to a promising future for the sutures market.</p></p>
