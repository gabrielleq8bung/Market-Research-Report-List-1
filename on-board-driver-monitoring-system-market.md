<p><strong>The On Board Driver Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active DMS</li><li>Passive DMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On Board Driver Monitoring System (DMS) market comprises two primary types: Active DMS and Passive DMS. Active DMS actively engages with the driver to assess alertness and attentiveness, employing tools like eye-tracking and facial recognition to detect signs of drowsiness or distraction, prompting corrective actions. In contrast, Passive DMS monitors driver behavior without direct intervention, utilizing algorithms to analyze patterns over time. Both types aim to enhance safety by ensuring drivers remain vigilant and reduce the risk of accidents.</p></p>
