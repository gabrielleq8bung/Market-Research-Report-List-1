<p><p>Laser coding and marking equipment encompasses three primary types: Fiber Lasers, CO2 Lasers, and UV Lasers. Fiber lasers are known for their efficiency and precision, making them ideal for industrial applications like metal marking. CO2 lasers effectively mark non-metal materials such as plastics and wood, offering versatility across various industries. UV lasers, with their fine focusing and minimal heat impact, are best suited for sensitive materials, ensuring high-quality marks on delicate substrates. Each type serves specific needs in marking and coding applications.</p></p>
