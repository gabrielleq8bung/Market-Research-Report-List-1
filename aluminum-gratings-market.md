<p><strong>The Aluminum Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press-locked Gratings</li><li>Swage-locked Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum gratings market consists of various types, notably press-locked and swage-locked gratings. Press-locked gratings utilize a manufacturing process where the surfaces are pressed together, creating a strong bond between the bars and the frame, suitable for pedestrian areas and heavy loads. Conversely, swage-locked gratings involve the mechanical swaging of the cross bars into the bearing bars, providing enhanced strength and stability, making them ideal for industrial applications with higher load requirements. Both types offer corrosion resistance and lightweight properties.</p></p>
