<p><strong>The Two Wheeler Anti-lock Braking Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-channel ABS</li><li>Multi-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Two Wheeler Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) market is primarily divided into two types: Single-channel ABS and Multi-channel ABS. </p><p>Single-channel ABS typically controls only the front wheel, making it more cost-effective and suitable for entry-level bikes. In contrast, Multi-channel ABS manages both front and rear wheels independently, enhancing stability and control during braking, which is crucial for high-performance and premium motorcycles. The choice between these systems often depends on the vehicle type, intended use, and rider's needs.</p></p>
