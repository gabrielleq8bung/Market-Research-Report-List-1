Amorphous silicon technology utilizes thin-film materials to create sensors that are cost-effective and suitable for a range of applications, including thermal imaging and surveillance. Vanadium oxide technology, on the other hand, leverages the unique properties of vanadium oxide to achieve higher sensitivity and better performance at varying temperatures, making it ideal for industrial and military applications. Both technologies cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
