<p><p>The non-contact multi-function infrared thermometer market includes two primary types: forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead type thermometers measure body temperature from a distance by detecting infrared radiation from the forehead, making them hygienic and quick to use, ideal for mass screenings. Ear type thermometers, on the other hand, measure temperature via the ear canal using infrared sensors, providing accurate readings but requiring direct contact. Both types cater to varying consumer needs in healthcare, home use, and public health settings.</p></p>
