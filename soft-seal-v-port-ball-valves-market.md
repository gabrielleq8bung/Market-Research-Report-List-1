<p><p>The soft seal V port ball valves market is primarily categorized into two types: flanged and flangeless valves. Flanged valves feature a raised face or flat surface with bolt holes for secure attachment to piping systems, ensuring easy installation and maintenance. In contrast, flangeless valves are designed without flange connections, enabling direct integration into compact spaces and reducing weight. Both types cater to various industrial applications, offering precise flow control and enhanced sealing performance suitable for different operational environments.</p></p>
