<p><p>Spring loaded check valves are mechanical devices designed to prevent backflow in pipelines by utilizing a spring mechanism that holds the valve disc closed until the pressure of the fluid in the forward direction exceeds that of the spring. They are commonly used in various industries, including oil and gas, water and wastewater management, and chemical processing, due to their efficiency and reliability in maintaining fluid flow in one direction.</p><p>The Spring Loaded Check Valves Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
